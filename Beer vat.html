Beer vat

Question

A 500 gallon beer vat contains 4% alcohol (by volume). Beer with 6% alcohol is pumped into the vat at a rate of 5 gal/min and the mixtured is pumped out at the same rate. What is the alcohol percentage after 1 hour?

Variable Definition
percentage of alcohol in tank p(t)
rate of alcohol change 5dpdt
alcohol in .06(5)=.3
alcohol out p(t)100(5)=.05p(t)
rate of alcohol change alcohol in - alcohol out
5dpdt=.3−.05p1.6−.01pdp=dt∫1.6−.01pdp=∫dt−100ln|.06−.01p|=t+C
Solve for C Let p(0)=4 (4% alcohol when starting)
−100ln(.06−.01(4))=0+CC=391.2
∴−100ln(.06−.01p)=t+391.2
Let t=60 (1 hour)
−100ln(.06−.01p)=60+391.2ln(.06−.01p)=−4.512eln(.06−.01p)=e−4.512.06−.01p=e−4.512p=.06−e−4.512.01p≈4.9%

Answer

After 1 hour there is 4.9% alcohol in the vat.
